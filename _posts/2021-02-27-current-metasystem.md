## The Metasystem 2021: Review

**游빐 LEGEND:**
- 游릭 Denotes Beeminder goals that are *going really well*, and are at a point where I enjoy doing them or do them automatically.
- 游리 Denotes Beeminder goals that I'm *having difficulty with*, but still manage to get done when needed.
- 游댮 Denotes Beeminder goals that I'm either *often derailing on*, or putting off altogether due to friction somewhere.
- 游닇 Denotes Beeminder goals that are *outcome-oriented*, meaning they track finished, tangible products in some manner.
- 游대 Denotes Beeminder goals that are *systems-oriented*, meaning they track daily habits that contribute to outcomes, but can be done endlessly.
- 游꾿 Denotes Beeminder goals that are *miscellaneous* or meta in nature, or just for fun.
